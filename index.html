HDB Price Prediction (WIP)

December 19, 2018

The HDB Resale Price dataset contains information on 785,798 HDB flats that were resold between January 1990 and October 2018. The dataset was compiled by the Singapore Government and is frequently being updated. The purpose of this project is to utilise regression techniques to understand what are the main drivers of HDB prices.

Recommender System for DonorsChoose.org

November 29, 2018

(Image Source: mita.gov.mt)

According to DonorsChoose.org, many classrooms in the US do not have sufficient funds to allow their students to learn effectively. Consequently, many teachers end up forking out their own money to fund classroom activities. In order to manage and resolve this predicament, DonorsChoose.org was set up as a platform for schools and teachers to reach out to the general populace to seek fundings.

The organisation provides rich anonymised datasets (2012-2018) on Kaggle and invites the community to help them pair up donation projects to existing and new donors alike. The winning solution will be implemented in email campaigns.
